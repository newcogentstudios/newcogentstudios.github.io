Privacy Policy

Introduction

This application ("the App") is designed with user privacy as a core principle. The App does not collect, store, or transmit personal data to external servers.

Data Collection

The App does not collect any personal information from users.

Local Data Processing

The App may request access to photos and media stored on your device. This access is used solely to enable core features of the App.

All data processing is performed locally on your device. No images, media, or related data are transmitted to any external servers.

Data Sharing

The App does not share any user data with third parties.

Data Storage

Any data used by the App remains stored locally on the user’s device and is not transferred outside the device.

Security

Since all processing occurs locally on the device and no data is transmitted externally, the risk of data exposure through the App is minimized.

Third-Party Services

The App does not use third-party services that collect user data.

Children’s Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any personal data from children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Users are encouraged to review it periodically.
